The gap in spatial computing

Content, face, and lens are funded. What's being built is not. Construction Tech and 3D vision for the built environment is the empty slot. Polymetron fills it with a reasoning layer that survives technical due diligence — every claim cites real code, real zoning, real public data.

Built on a working food-system platform deployed at Georgia Tech and the City of Atlanta (30+ live data layers, real institutional partnerships). The construction vertical is the next module on the same fabric.
